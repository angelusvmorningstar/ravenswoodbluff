Into the Woods

A curated Blood on the Clocktower script set deep in a haunted forest – one character roster, one night order, one coherent world.

The Premise

Most scripts for Blood on the Clocktower are designed to work across any setting the Storyteller imagines. Into the Woods is different. It is a single, fixed script built around a specific place: a forest that has been wrong for a long time, a village on its edge that does not know what it is harbouring, and a night order tuned to create a particular kind of paranoia.

The characters were chosen to reinforce each other and the setting. They reward experienced play but are not inaccessible to newcomers. The Storyteller notes explain not just what each character does, but why it belongs here and what the game wants from it.

What Is Included

  • A hand-picked character roster drawn from across the official Blood on the Clocktower releases.
  • An original night order tuned to the specific rhythm this script wants to produce.
  • Full Storyteller notes: per-character guidance, the session arc to aim for, and common bluffing traps to watch.
  • Alternative-art character icons from the Ravenswood Bluff icon set, sized and formatted for print.
  • A recommended player count range and setup notes for different group sizes.
